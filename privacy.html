Your data matters

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and keep your fitness journey, contact details, and membership information protected.

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Information We Collect

Empire Fitness may collect your name, phone number, email address, membership interest, fitness goals, BMI calculator inputs, and messages submitted through the contact form.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, recommend suitable membership plans, book consultations, provide gym updates, improve our services, and support your training experience.

Membership And Fitness Data

Any fitness-related details you share, including goals, progress, body measurements, or transformation stories, are used only to guide coaching, track progress, and improve your member experience.

Cookies And Website Analytics

Our website may use basic browser storage or analytics tools to understand site performance, improve page experience, and remember simple preferences such as dismissed promotional popups.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, manage enquiries, or deliver membership services.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect your information. No online system is completely risk-free, but we work to keep your data handled carefully and responsibly.

Your Choices

You may request updates, corrections, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You may also choose not to submit optional fitness or goal information through the website.
